# Junk Bonds vs Treasuries: Crisis Performance & Liquidity Risk

## Objective
Analyze why high-yield bonds underperform U.S. Treasuries during market stress
despite similar day-to-day volatility.

## Data & Tools
- Bloomberg Terminal (YAS, LQA, FIW)
- Excel
- Python (Pandas)

## Key Findings
- Liquidity deterioration drives outsized drawdowns
- Credit spread widening amplifies tail risk
- Flight-to-quality dominates during systemic shocks

## Periods Analyzed
- 2008 Financial Crisis
- 2020 COVID Shock
- 2022 Rate Hike Cycle
